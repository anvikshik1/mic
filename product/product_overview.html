Overview Products

Amplifier Research

AR RF/Microwave Instrumentation is the World-Class Source For: RF Conducted Immunity Systems, Radiated Immunity Test Systems, Broadband High Power, Solid State, RF and Microwave Amplifiers, TWT Amplifiers, Log Periodic and High-Gain Horn Antennas, EMC Test Equipment, Hybrid RF Power Amplifier Modules, Software, Field Probes and Monitors, Electromagnetic Safety Products, EMC Accessories, EMC Test Software and Hybrid RF Power Amplifier Modules.

RF & EMC Products

Amplifiers

All our RF solid-state amplifiers have modulation capability that will faithfully reproduce AM, FM or Pulse Modulation appearing on the input signal for use in the most demanding EMC applications. These self-contained, broadband, completely solid state amplifiers are designed for applications requiring the ultimate in output power over a wide instantaneous bandwidth with high gain.

Antennas

AR offers a wide range of high power, log periodic, high-gain horn, and bent element antennas, and more. With antennas available up to 50 GHz and 20,000 W of input CW power, our innovative antennas offer features available exclusively from AR.

Field Measuring

AR offers a complete selection of test accessories that give you the most reliable results, such as probes, software, system controllers, couplers, and more. Many even make testing quicker, more efficient, and more accurate. They're all matched to our amplifiers to make your setup as easy as possible.

Test Systems

Test systems by AR can deliver a solution that integrates all your testing needs for radiated and conducted immunity, radiated and conducted emissions, and more. With a highly experienced team, we have the expertise to supply fully automated systems needed to test various EMC standards.
