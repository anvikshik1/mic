Overview Applications

Meeting the Demands of Today's Test and Measurement Applications

The test and measurement equipment market presents increasing demand for electronics, automation, connectivity in automotive, aerospace and defense, industrial transportation and manufacturing, IT/telecommunications, and other sectors.

Maxim Instruments products and solutions can enable you to measure content, voltage, and other parameters quickly and accurately as the precision of electronics continue to increase. Our complete solutions are designed to meet your ever-changing market needs.

Market Trends in Growth Industries

The trends in growing markets with increasing demands and cutting-edge solutions for telecommunications technologies in communications, automotive, and defense are creating challenges for the test and measurement industry.

Automotive

Integrating more connectivity into vehicles, data and cellular networks, and infrastructure increases the demand for electronic testing devices.

The demand for high speed and more bandwidths keeps growing as the need for data analytics also continues to rise.

Aerospace

Aerospace electronics and avionics testing, and measurement is essential to ensure safety in aircraft and reliability and efficiency in communication systems, navigation systems, instrument landing systems and more.

Aerospace electronics and avionics will continue to advance, with high-speed, high-bandwidth, data-intensive functions driving demand for advanced test solution

Internet of Things

Connected devices is driving a growing need for inspecting, testing, and verifying smart products and other equipment to ensure that they work correctly once deployed.

Ensure optimum performance with precision, interoperability, security, and scalability.

Data Center

Demand for electronic test and measurement equipment to address network function and health, product development, real-time analytics, and troubleshooting is on the rise.

As bandwidth demands rise, new standards and higher data-transmission speeds will result in the need for high-speed digital testing equipment.
