Overview Industries

The Importance of Test and Measurement

With an expected market growth from "$25.7 billion in 2018 to $32.3 billioby 2024, at a CAGR of 3.90%", the test and measurement equipment industry has established paramount importance and demand. The need for testing and measurement is in almost every industry-ranging from automotive, IT and telecommunications, and aerospace and defense to electronics, industrial and healthcare.

At Maxim Instruments our customers can rent and buy test equipment from automatic test equipment (ATE), radio frequency (RF) test and data acquisition (DAQ) to oscilloscopes, signal generators and spectrum analyzers. We have extensive experience offering products for a variety of test and measurement applications.

Why is it Significant?

We live in a world where we are surrounded by electronics devices to digitally enhanced equipment. We use electrical devices for every little thing that we do, but when there is greatness there will be difficulties. We have become so used to of digital and electrical gadgets that when they stop working, we become distressed. Therefore, Test and Measurement equipment is crucial for safety compliance testing, as well as for the testing and analysis of the performance of new products in the market.

Aviation

Aviation is a high-tech industry that drives innovation and plays a key part in the economy. The development of safe and efficient measurement technology is the bedrock for successful ground and flight testing in the aviation industry.

Automotive

The automobile industry has witnessed a continuous increase in the integration of electronics architecture in vehicles. The rising demand for safety and comfort features has led to huge innovations and R&D efforts from OEMs and automotive suppliers for the testing of electronics components and compliance with government regulations.

Medical

The rising demand for test and measurement equipment for medical devices and renewed significance in developing diagnostic tests is accelerating the growth of the test and measurement equipment market for the healthcare sector.

Electronics

Used for the development and manufacturing of products across industries, deployment of networks, and maintenance of services, electronic test and measurement is essential for performance and validation of electric and electronic devices. Safety testing in the electronic industry is crucial to avoid health hazards and damage.

Education and Research

Increased demand and advancements in biomedical research activities require education and research institutions to provide laboratory test equipment. Government funding to various institutions for improved quality in education and research is a major growth factor for laboratory test equipment.
