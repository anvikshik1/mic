Testing and Measuring in the Lab

Different types of test and measurement equipment are used to analyze, validate, and verify measurements of electronic and mechanical systems. Electronics, research labs, material analysis and manufacturing all use test and measurement equipment to measure various components of the materials they are working with and analyzing.

Maxim Instruments offers electronic test and measurement equipment for rent and purchase. Our products set standards in research, development, production, and service. As a key industry partner, we offer a broad spectrum of market leading solutions.

Test and Measurement Instruments and Solutions

Perform product reliability tests in avionics, military applications, electronics, and consumer goods, including standards set forth by the Department of Defense and the International Electrotechnical Commission.

With advancements in autonomous driving technology and recent Internet of Things (IoT) technology trends, there is a need for improved bandwidth and latency reduction, which can be easily achieved through 5G. Additionally, the shift toward connected cars and intelligent transportation systems, along with increasing demand for medical devices in the wake of COVID-19, is expected to drive the test and measurement equipment market further.

EMC/EMI Field Test Solutions

Perform pre-compliance testing to prevent interference of products and systems from spurious emission and absorption of EMI.

RF/Microwave

RF test solutions for immunity and wireless testing with all the power you need.

Test RF and microwave devices which require highly configurable systems that can meet your specific test needs.

Power and Test

Support high end engineering applications that require extreme reliability and performance.

Choose from application specific power and compliance test solutions for engineers that need differentiated power products and services.

Environmental

Ensure that your product meets consumer expectations and industry guidelines. Accelerate reliability testing that helps you meet and beat industry test standards.

Identify design flaws and establish the range of temperatures at which the product will reliably operate.
