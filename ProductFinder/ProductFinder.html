AE Techron 7224 Power Amplifier DC-300 kHz 900W

Key Features

  • Up to 1000 watts RMS Power output
  • Small signal response up to 1 MHz
  • Can be user to create series or parellel system for up to 3 times the voltage or 4 times of the current output.
  • up to 16A at 13.5 VDC

Description

AE Techron 7224 Power Amplifier DC - 300 kHz 900W is a 4-quadrant power amplifier that offers the features of larger industrial amplifiers in a smaller 2U size that runs from single phase 120/230VAC. It features DC - 400 kHz bandwidth, controlled-current or controlled-voltage modes of operation and has a wide range of field-configurable options.

The 7224's configurability and flexibility have made it the choice of many research and university labs. It can be field-configured to run well into loads from 100 ohms to as low as 0.5 ohms. Even with its small bench top size, the 7224 can produce as much as 50 amps of current and as much as 158 volts, providing serious power for a wide range of applications and experiments. With its 75V/uS slew rate, it can accurately reproduce wave forms from DC to above 400 kHz, making it a great choice for high-speed control applications.
